Frequently Asked Questions

What is ToxiScan and how does it work?

ToxiScan is an AI-powered content analysis platform that evaluates text for potentially toxic or harmful language. Our machine learning model analyzes submitted text and provides a confidence score on whether the content is safe or potentially problematic, helping content moderators and businesses ensure their platforms remain safe and welcoming.

How accurate is ToxiScan's analysis?

ToxiScan uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on diverse datasets to provide high accuracy. However, as with any AI system, it may not be 100% accurate in all cases. Our models typically achieve over 95% accuracy on standard benchmarks, and we continuously improve our models based on user feedback and new data to enhance performance.

Is my data secure when using ToxiScan?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All text submissions are encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. We do not store your submitted content longer than necessary for analysis, and we never share your data with third parties. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

How can I integrate ToxiScan with my application?

ToxiScan offers a robust API for seamless integration with your applications. Our API documentation provides detailed instructions on authentication, endpoints, request parameters, and response formats. We offer SDKs for popular programming languages including Python, JavaScript, and Java. For enterprise integration support, please contact our sales team.
